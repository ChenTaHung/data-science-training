5.1 Objectives & Resources

  • learn ggplot2 with mpg dataframe (important to play with other data than your own, you’ll learn something.)
  • practice writing a script (maybe call it mpg_viz.R?)
  • practice rstudio-github workflow
  • use and credit http://r4ds.had.co.nz/data-visualisation.html

Why do we start with data viz? Not only is data viz a big part of analysis, it’s a way to SEE your progress as you learn to code. “ggplot2 implements the grammar of graphics, a coherent system for describing and building graphs. With ggplot2, you can do more faster by learning one system and applying it in many places.” - R4DS
